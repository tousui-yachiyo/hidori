The Name

Two characters: Hi, and Tori.

Two characters, each carrying the story of a place called Hagi.

A Story from Hagi

Hagi, in Yamaguchi — white-walled streets and a stillness that lingers.
Its rice, its water, and the hands that brought them together: from these, HIDORI was born.

Pour a glass, and the aroma unfolds; one sip, and a landscape lights up within.
A moment of Hagi, carried into your evening.
